Register Machines and Compilation
Relevant sections in text: 5.1, 5.2, 5.4., 5.5

This assignment covers the three major programs introduced in chapter
5 -- register-machine simulator, explicit-control evaluator and compiler.
There is a lot a lot of actual programming required here, and it can be
done without mastering the details of these three programs.
Nevertheless, students will be dealing wit a lot of code and they will
need time to assimilate it.
